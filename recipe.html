叉烧蛋炒饭

English Name: Char Siu Egg Fried Rice

Variations of this dish can be found mostly in the Chinese province, Guangdong (广东), due to the main ingredient in this dish (叉烧 English: Char Siu) already being a Cantonese-style roasted meat. The eggs in this dish are usually scrambled, mixed in a wok with rice, garlic, garnish, and other sauces to make up the flavor. It can be served as any meal (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner), maybe served with some vegetables on the side (ex. 油菜).

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil or (Duck or Pork) Lard
  • Minced Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Rice
  • Diced Char Siu
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Fish Sauce
  • Scallions

Instructions

  • In a bowl, add eggs and salt, then mix.
  • In a wok, add your vegetable oil (or lard).
  • Add your minced garlic.
  • Stir the garlic so it isn't clumped together.
  • Add your eggs.
  • Add your rice.
  • Mix them together.
  • Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and fish sauce.
  • Mix more.
  • Add your diced Char Siu.
  • Mix again.
  • When the fire is off, add scallions.
